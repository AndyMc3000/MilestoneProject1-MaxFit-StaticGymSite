Your Club. Your Way.

Your long-term health and fitness is at the heart of what we do. Our mission is to maximise the potential within each of our Club members.

We are a passionate and dedicated team of multi-diciplinary fitness professionals. By emplying state-of-the-art equipment, best in class training and wellbeing techniques, nutrition and meal planning, we can ensure our members can work towards achievable and meaningful fitness goals. Whatever age, or level of fitness you are at, we can advise you on a fitness program tailored specifically for you.

Talk to us to today to start your journey to a healthier and happier you!

What We Do

Our Gym

At MAXFIT we source the best gym equipment for our members. Our Gym is laid out across 3 split-level floors, which are surrounded by inspirational views of the Killarney lakes. Each flor is dedicated to one of three training diciplines, those being; Cardio machines, Upper & Lower body machines, and lifting & free weights. Our Classes

We have a wide variety of classes on offer at MAXFIT. We launch a new class timetable very 2 months and we try to make classes available based on demand. Our teachers are experts in their field and are available to support beginners and experienced members alike. From heated yoga to shredded HIIT training, you’ll find a class to suit your time and performance level.

Personal Training

Health and fitness is our passion, and this shows in the professional approach our Personal Trainers take to helping our clients achieve results they never thought were possible. From bespoke training plans to meal planning, We will guide you and teach you how to navigate through the ever changing world of exercise and nutrition, to ensure you achieve your own 'MAXFIT'!
